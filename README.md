# UGI Corporation

UGI Corporation is a Fortune 500 international energy distribution and services company headquartered in King of Prussia, Pennsylvania. UGI distributes natural gas, liquid propane, and electricity through four business segments: UGI Utilities (natural gas and electric distribution in Pennsylvania), AmeriGas Propane (largest US propane marketer), Midstream and Marketing, and UGI International (European energy distribution across 17 countries). No public developer API has been identified; the company primarily uses EDI and utility industry standards for B2B data exchange.
